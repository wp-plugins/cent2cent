charge for your text, images and videos.

== Description ==

Cent2Cent provides a flexible e-content monetization platform that allows website owners to charge for any content they have. 

The Cent2Cent system is unique because it works for all types of content, and can be customized to allow for a variety of subscription options. 

The system is easy to incorporate into your website, taking just 15 minutes to install. 

Cent2Cent works well for individuals and small businesses, saving them money by aggregating all payments at the end of the month. 

But Cent2Cent isn’t only for individuals and small websites; corporations including NBC and Israeli media group Mako have used Cent2Cent to stream content on their sites.

The Cent2Cent Platform provides a WordPress Plugin for easy install.

Start making money from what you love.

Cent2Cent - Content Into Profit. Easy.


== Installation ==

1. Upload Cent2Vent For WordPress to your wp-content/plugins directory.
2. Activate it in "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
3. Set your credentials at the Plugins menus -> Cent2Cent
